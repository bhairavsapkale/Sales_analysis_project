# Sales_analysis_project
🚀 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing business sales data to uncover actionable insights that can improve decision-making. Using Power BI, I built an interactive dashboard to track key performance indicators, identify trends, and evaluate sales performance across regions, products, and channels.
The goal of this project was to simulate a real-world business analyst role, where raw data is transformed into meaningful insights for stakeholders.
🎯 Problem Statement
Businesses often struggle to:
Track sales performance across multiple dimensions
Identify underperforming regions or products
Understand customer behavior and seasonal trends
This dashboard solves these challenges by providing a centralized, visual analytics solution.
📌 Key Features
📍 Region-wise Sales Analysis
👨‍💼 Salesperson Performance Tracking
📅 Monthly Sales Trend (Seasonality Analysis)
🛒 Product Category Insights
🌐 Online vs Offline Channel Comparison
🎯 Interactive Filters (State, City, Category, Customer Type, Date)
📊 Key Insights
Total Sales Performance:
Gross Sales: 128M
Net Sales: 115M
Avg Revenue per Order: 115K
Regional Insights:
East & North regions are top performers (~31M each)
West region shows comparatively lower sales → improvement opportunity
Sales Trends:
Peak sales observed in May–June
Dip in March and Oct–Nov, indicating seasonality
Sales Channel:
Online contributes ~53% of total revenue → key growth driver
Product Performance:
Office Supplies show consistent high sales
Electronics show fluctuating demand
Sales Team Performance:
Top performers outperform others significantly → scope for training and optimization
